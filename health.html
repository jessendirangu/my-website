What is Cancer?
Cancer is a disease where abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the body.

Common Causes:
Smoking, poor diet, lack of exercise, infections, genetics.

Early Warning Signs:

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Persistent fatigue
  • Unusual bleeding
  • Lumps or swelling

Prevention:

  • Healthy diet
  • Regular exercise
  • Screening tests
  • Avoid smoking & alcohol

Treatment Options:

  • Surgery
  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiotherapy
  • Immunotherapy

Early detection saves lives. Always seek medical advice if symptoms persist.

🥗 Healthy Diet

Healthy Food

Fresh Vegetables

✅ Foods to Eat

  • 🥦 Vegetables (broccoli, spinach, carrots)
  • 🍎 Fruits (apples, bananas, berries)
  • 🍗 Lean protein (chicken, fish, beans)
  • 🥛 Dairy (milk, yogurt)
  • 🌾 Whole grains (oats, brown rice)
  • 💧 Drink plenty of water

⚠️ Foods to Avoid

  • 🍔 Fast foods
  • 🍟 Fried foods
  • 🥤 Sugary drinks
  • 🍩 Processed snacks
  • 🚬 Smoking & alcohol

Healthy eating reduces the risk of cancer, heart disease, and diabetes.
