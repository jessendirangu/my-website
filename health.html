Hypertension

Causes: Stress, high salt diet

Symptoms: Headaches, dizziness

Prevention: Exercise, reduce salt

Diabetes

Causes: High sugar intake

Symptoms: Frequent urination, fatigue

Prevention: Healthy diet, exercise

Early Warning Signs

• Persistent fatigue

• Sudden weight changes

• Shortness of breath
