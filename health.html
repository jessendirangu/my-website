What is Cancer?
Cancer is a disease where abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the body.

Common Causes:
Smoking, poor diet, lack of exercise, infections, genetics.

Warning Signs:

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Bleeding
  • Lumps

Prevention:

  • Healthy lifestyle
  • Screening
  • No smoking
