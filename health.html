What is Cancer?
Cancer is a disease where abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in the body.

Common Causes:
Smoking, poor diet, lack of exercise, infections, genetics.

Early Warning Signs:

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Persistent fatigue
  • Unusual bleeding
  • Lumps or swelling

Prevention:

  • Healthy diet
  • Regular exercise
  • Screening tests
  • Avoid smoking & alcohol

🥗 Healthy Diet

Eat More:

  • Fruits & vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Lean protein (fish, chicken)
  • Water

Avoid:

  • Processed foods
  • Too much sugar
  • Fast food
  • Excess alcohol
