Cancer Awareness

What is Cancer?

Cancer is a condition where abnormal cells grow uncontrollably and may spread to other parts of the body.

Causes & Risk Factors

  • Smoking and tobacco use
  • Poor diet and obesity
  • Lack of exercise
  • Alcohol consumption
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Exposure to harmful chemicals

Common Types of Cancer

  • Breast cancer
  • Lung cancer
  • Prostate cancer
  • Cervical cancer
  • Colorectal cancer

Signs and Symptoms

  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Persistent fatigue
  • Lumps or swelling
  • Chronic pain
  • Unusual bleeding

Screening & Diagnosis

  • Blood tests
  • Biopsy
  • X-ray, CT scan, MRI
  • Pap smear and mammogram

Prevention

  • Healthy balanced diet
  • Regular exercise
  • Avoid smoking
  • Limit alcohol intake
  • Regular medical checkups

Treatment Options

  • Surgery
  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiotherapy
  • Immunotherapy
  • Targeted therapy

Side Effects of Treatment

  • Fatigue
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Hair loss
  • Weak immune system

Patient Support & Care

  • Emotional support
  • Nutrition and hydration
  • Pain management
  • Family support

When to Seek Urgent Help

  • Severe persistent pain
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
  • Rapid weight loss
Early detection saves lives. Regular screening is essential.
